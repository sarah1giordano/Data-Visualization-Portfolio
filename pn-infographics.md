## About
I developed these infographics building upon the initial design by Olivia Iles for the FY2024 grantees and previous iterations of grantee infographics developed by previous Promise Neighborhood technical assistance teams. Following initial mockups, I worked with Westat's internal graphics team to develop publication-ready visualizations in line with Promise Neighborhood's overarching style guide.
The infographic below is an example of the final template, populated with data for Bowling Green's Promise Neighborhood. The infographics can additionally be found at the [Department of Education's archived Promise Neighborhood site](https://promiseneighborhoods.ed.gov/infographics).
Please note if viewing the full Department of Education infographic site that I contributed to infographics of sites labelled as **FY 2024**.
