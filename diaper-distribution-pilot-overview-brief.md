## About
I developed this brief and the key visuals below in partnership with Monirah Al-Abdulmunem, Marneena Evans, and Allison Hyra. I first mocked up visuals in Excel. Following this, I worked with Westat's internal graphics team to develop publication-ready visualizations in line with our project's overarching style guide. The full brief is available on the [Administration for Children and Families Diaper Distribution Pilot webpage](https://acf.gov/sites/default/files/documents/opre/DDDRP-GrantOverviewBrief_final---updated-September-2024-3.pdf).
